About Us

We are a unique NGO committed to fighting drug abuse. Through dynamic youth programs, parent education and engagement with key community stakeholders, AFTI is dedicated to the promotion of a healthy community, physical wellness and the prevention of alcohol and drug abuse. We envision a healthy community where youth choose not to use DRUGS.
